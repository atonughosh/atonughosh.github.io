01 — About

Systems engineering
where hardware meets the network.

I'm a final-semester PhD scholar in Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, in the Smart Wireless Applications & Networking (SWAN) Lab under Prof. Sudip Misra, and a former Research Intern at Samsung R&D Institute India, Bangalore. My work lives in the resource-constrained middle where embedded hardware, networks and machine learning meet.

Over 6+ years I've taken systems from breadboard to production: HTTP and a full TLS 1.3 handshake carried over LoRa for remote and ad-hoc networks, a 4×4 and 8x8 LoRa MIMO testbed for higher throughput, condition monitoring for overhead cranes and rotating machinery, and a lightweight blockchain running on a microcontroller. Several are patented and some are in production.

I've led small teams on consulting and research projects for Tata Steel, Bokaro Steel Plant, Haldia Water Services, and CPHEEO resulting in end-to-end systems and their field validation. My aim always has been and still remains to build systems that are reliable, low-power, and genuinely useful to the people who depend on them.

Different projects, one throughline: making capable systems run on tiny, far-flung, power-starved hardware — and keeping them secure and maintainable once they ship.

Long-range, low-power links that carry more than telemetry — HTTP and TLS over LoRa, message handling, interference management, and MIMO for throughput.

Industrial deployment In production

EOT Crane Condition Monitoring

Tata Steel Downstream Products Ltd — Jamshedpur, India

Led a team of four to build and deploy a full-stack condition-monitoring system for Electric Overhead Travelling cranes — real-time vibration, temperature and electrical sensing streamed to plant-floor dashboards. Patented and running in production.

Flagship research Patented · arXiv

HTTP & TLS over LoRa

Siia - SWAN Lab, IIT Kharagpur, India

A transparent proxy that accepts ordinary HTTPS from a Wi-Fi device, carries the request across a LoRa backhaul, and completes a full end-to-end TLS 1.3 handshake at the far end — unlocking the web for remote and ad-hoc networks with no cellular coverage.

ESP32RFM95WRaspberry PiNginxTLS 1.3Python
Research system Patented · arXiv

LoRa MIMO Testbed

4×4 spatial multiplexing — SWAN Lab, IIT Kharagpur, India

Designed and evaluated a 4×4 MIMO system for LoRa that uses spatial multiplexing to exchange parallel data streams, pushing effective throughput well past a single link while keeping LoRa's low-power profile intact.

ESP32RFM95WMicroPythonSpatial MUX
Gov-sponsored deployment In use

Water Distribution Pressure Monitoring

Haldia Water Services · CPHEEO, Govt. of India

A solar-powered, remotely configurable IoT node that streams domestic water-pipe pressure to the cloud for ML analysis — built for a CPHEEO / Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs project and deployed in the field.

Bokaro Steel Plant, India

Led a team of three to retrofit 4–20 mA Honeywell gas sensors and relay their readings over LoRaWAN to a central server — bringing decades-old industrial instrumentation online without ripping it out and replacing it.
